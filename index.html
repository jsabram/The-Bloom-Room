About us

Then...

The Blooming Room was firstly established in early 2000s. Back then, we were working only during the spring and summer period. Our flower compositions were noticed by a local photographer, who then wanted to include some of our decorations in his photoshoots. Thanks to it, our flower shop became immensly popular.

...Today

Now, more than 20 years later, The Blooming Bloom is one of the most renowed florists shops in the world. Our craft has been mentioned in famous magazines and we received many orders from all over the planet. We focus on constantly improving our work and delivering flowers that will make any occassion unforgettable.

All of our employees are certified

We have won over 100 florist contests

5% of every sold item is given to a local dog shelter
